About Me

My name is Veronika Sopov, I’m 28 years old and it’s the second year I live in Batumi, Georgia. I was born in Russia and made 'Aliah' with my family when I was 3. We lived in Ashkelon this whole time until I decided to move with my husband to Georgia.
In Israel I served the army for 3 years, worked at call center of the fire department and graduated bachelor’s degree in psychology, criminology and political science.

One led to another, I met my husband, we got to Georgia and just fell for this place. The nature, the calmness, hospitality and of course all the dogs on the street captured my heart so we decided to stay a bit longer.
In this website I’d like to show you few amazing places in Georgia.

Georgia

The Green Lake Mountains

Georgia, or as the local call it Sakartvelo, is a picturesque country at the crossroads of Europe and Asia, known for its breathtaking mountains and diverse landscapes. Tbilisi, the capital, features a charming mix of old and new architecture. The nation has a rich culinary tradition, famous for dishes like khachapuri and unique wines. Georgia's ancient history is reflected in its many UNESCO World Heritage sites. With warm hospitality and vibrant culture, it’s a captivating destination to explore.
